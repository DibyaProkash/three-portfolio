About Me

Who I Am?

I'm Dibya Prokash Sarkar, a passionate Full Stack Developer, HCI Researcher, and Educator based in Canada. I hold a Master of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Victoria, British Columbia, where I specialized in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Data Visualization research. I am deeply passionate about building dynamic, user-centered web and mobile applications and leveraging research-driven insights to improve the way people interact with technology.

My career goal is to continue developing impactful software solutions that blend robust technical design with meaningful human experiences. Academically, I aspire to pursue a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction and Information Science, with a particular focus on understanding generative and explainable AI and its transformative impact across all paradigms of our society.
